What is the check-in and check-out times?

+

Check-in is from 4:00 PM, and check-out is by 10:00 AM. We're happy to consider different times depending on availability — just let us know in advance.

Is the entire property private, or will there be other guests during our stay?

+

The villa is entirely private and reserved exclusively for your stay. You will not share any spaces with other guests - your time here is yours alone.

Is the pool heated? Is it open all year round?

+

The pool is not heated, but it remains accessible throughout the entire year. It is also equipped with a counter-current swimming system, allowing you to swim comfortably at any time.

Are pets allowed at the property?

+

We love animals, but to ensure the comfort of all our guests and to preserve the unique character of the villa, pets are not allowed on the property. We truly appreciate your understanding.

Is there on-site parking? How many cars can be accommodated?

+

Yes, the property offers private on-site parking for up to 4 vehicles. Additionally, an electric vehicle charging station is available for guests who wish to recharge their car during their stay.

Is the house suitable for children or guests with reduced mobility?

+

The villa is family-friendly, but due to its architectural features and outdoor layout, it may not be fully accessible for guests with limited mobility. We're happy to provide more details based on your specific needs.

What amenities are included in the stay?

+

Your stay includes Wi-Fi, air conditioning/heating, high-quality linens and towels, a fully equipped kitchen, and private pool access. Upon request, we may offer additional touches to make your stay even more special.
