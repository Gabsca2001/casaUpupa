A Tuscan home where time slows down

Casa Upupa is a private retreat nestled in the heart of the Tuscan countryside, surrounded by olive trees, rolling hills, and the scent of Mediterranean herbs. Just minutes from the charming village of Bolgheri and the coast, it's a peaceful place designed for those who want to disconnect, slow down, and reconnect with what matters. The house blends contemporary lines with natural materials, creating a quiet elegance that feels both timeless and deeply rooted in its surroundings.

Bright spaces designed to welcome

The house opens toward the landscape with wide, sunlit interiors and large windows that invite the outside in. From the terrace, the view stretches toward the Tyrrhenian Sea and the island of Elba, shifting gently with the light throughout the day. Inside, six double bedrooms with private ensuite bathrooms accommodate up to twelve guests. Every room is designed with warmth and intention — simple yet refined spaces that promote rest, comfort, and a sense of connection.

A natural, contemporary style

Casa Upupa spans 470 square meters and is built around the idea of essential beauty. Natural materials such as wood, stone, and linen define the interiors, while clean, modern lines keep the atmosphere uncluttered and serene. The house includes two living areas, a fully equipped kitchen, a laundry room, and a quiet nook for reading or relaxing. Every corner reflects an understated elegance, in harmony with the surrounding landscape.

A place to return to

This is a home meant for slow mornings and long evenings. Large windows and sliding doors open the house to the outdoors, creating a seamless flow between inside and out. Whether you're traveling with family or friends, Casa Upupa offers space, privacy, and a calm rhythm. Like the hoopoe bird that returns here to nest each year, guests often find themselves drawn back — to rest, to be inspired, and to feel truly at home.
