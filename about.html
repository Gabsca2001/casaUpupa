Surrounded by Mediterranean nature

Casa Upupa is nestled in a Mediterranean garden where olive trees, lavender, rosemary, and wild herbs grow freely. The house blends seamlessly into this landscape, offering outdoor spaces that invite calm and presence. Every path, every shaded spot, every scent encourages a slower pace and a deeper connection with nature.

Open views, true silence

From the terrace, the view stretches far across the sea and the surrounding hills. The light changes throughout the day, casting different moods over the landscape. Here, silence is more than the absence of noise — it's the presence of space, of stillness, of time to think and breathe.

Comfort under the sky

Outdoor living at Casa Upupa is effortless. Guests can enjoy a barbecue area for long summer dinners, a refreshing outdoor shower, and a private covered parking area. Scattered around the garden are quiet corners under the trees, perfect for reading, resting, or simply watching the day unfold.

A pool with an infinity view

The pool opens out toward the horizon, with wide, uninterrupted views over the Tuscan hills and the nearby village of Casale Marittimo. Surrounded by nature and silence, it's a peaceful spot for morning swims, long afternoons in the sun, or sunset aperitivos. With its open setting and breathtaking perspective, the pool feels like part of the landscape — a place to unwind, breathe, and take it all in.

Time just for you

Loungers, soft towels, shaded areas, and the rhythm of cicadas in the background — the poolside is designed as a private sanctuary. Whether you choose to read, nap, meditate, or simply do nothing at all, this is a place where you can truly slow down and enjoy the luxury of having time to yourself.
